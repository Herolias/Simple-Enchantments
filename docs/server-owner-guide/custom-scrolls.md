If you want to reward your players with a combination of enchantments or an enchantment above its normal max level, you can do that with a custom scroll. The blank custom scroll is a creative-inventory-only item that can be enchanted with Enchantment Scrolls, the `/enchant` command, or `/give --enchants` with enchantments up to level 100. Players can then apply those enchantments one after another to the same item or to different items. After the last enchantment is applied, the scroll is consumed.
