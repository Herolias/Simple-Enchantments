# Eagle's Eye

![Eagle's Eye scroll icon](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Herolias/Simple-Enchantments/dev/docs/media/enchantment-icons/eagles-eye.png)

Increases projectile damage by up to 25% based on distance.
