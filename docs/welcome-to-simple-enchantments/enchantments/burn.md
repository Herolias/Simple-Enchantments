# Burn

![Burn scroll icon](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Herolias/Simple-Enchantments/dev/docs/media/enchantment-icons/burn.png)

Sets target on fire (5 damage/s for 3s) and lets it drop cooked variants of its drops. Not combinable with Freeze.
