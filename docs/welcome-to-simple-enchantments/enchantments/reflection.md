# Reflection

![Reflection scroll icon](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Herolias/Simple-Enchantments/dev/docs/media/enchantment-icons/reflection.png)

Reflects 10% of blocked damage back to the attacker. Not combinable with Absorption.
