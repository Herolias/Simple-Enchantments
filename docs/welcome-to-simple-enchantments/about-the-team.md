We're Elias (Herolias/MineAndCraft) and Rita (Sorath), two CS students from Germany. We’ve both played Minecraft for years and have been hooked on Hytale since Day 1 of Early Access.

## About Elias

I’m 22 and spend most of my free time programming or gaming. I’ve been playing Minecraft since 2014; that’s actually what got me into computers in the first place! I had to learn how to use a Linux terminal pretty early on just to host my own servers (thanks to some help from my dad) and to optimize the game for the potato PC I had at the time.

Playing Hytale for the first time was incredible. It has a lot of the features I always wished Minecraft had. Since Hytale modding is designed to be more accessible, I decided to give it a try and see what I could create.

I handle the technical side of Simple Enchantments. Feel free to reach out if you find a bug, have a feature request, or if you’re a fellow developer looking for help implementing your own enchantments!


## About Rita

I'm a 22 year old Hobby-Artist, and I handle the creative side of our mod. Since the art I've done so far was exclusively drawing, I actually had no prior experiences with 3D modeling, so working with Blockbench to do the models for our mod was my first time to ever touch 3D art, and it's a lot of fun. If you have questions or suggestions about balancing, recipes or models, feel free to reach out to me!
