# Scroll of Cleansing

If you ever change your mind about an Enchantment on one of your Items, you can easily remove it via the Scroll of Cleansing. It unlocks at the 2nd Tier of the Enchantment Table and is found in every Category. 
![](../media/images/yv6ZLge-09d60db98d.png)
You can use the Scroll of Cleansing like you would use any other Scroll: Right or left click and then select an Item. After selecting an Item, a new UI appears, showing you all the Enchantments on the Item and letting you choose one to remove. By default, you will not get the Scroll back of the Enchantment you removed, but that can be changed in the config.
