If you ever change your mind about an enchantment on one of your items, you can remove it with the Scroll of Cleansing. It unlocks at Tier <!-- DOCSTAT:recipe.Scroll_Cleansing.tier -->2<!-- /DOCSTAT --> of the Enchanting Table and appears in every category.
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Herolias/Simple-Enchantments/main/docs/media/images/yv6ZLge-09d60db98d.png)
Use the Scroll of Cleansing like any other scroll: right-click or left-click, then select an item. A new UI will show every enchantment on that item and let you choose one to remove. By default, you <!-- DOCSTAT:config.returnEnchantmentOnCleanse;will -->will not<!-- /DOCSTAT --> receive the removed enchantment as a scroll, but server owners can change that in the config.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Herolias/Simple-Enchantments/main/docs/media/images/VZxpzNR-c734e89026.png)
