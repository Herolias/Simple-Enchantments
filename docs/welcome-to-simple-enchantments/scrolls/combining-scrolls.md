# Combining Scrolls

At the [Engraving Table](https://wiki.hytalemodding.dev/mod/simple-enchantments/engraving-table), you can combine multiple scrolls (up to <!-- DOCSTAT:config.maxEnchantmentsPerItem -->5<!-- /DOCSTAT --> by default) into one custom scroll that holds all of their enchantments.
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Herolias/Simple-Enchantments/main/docs/media/images/4YNHRTT-ea75efb9b4.png)
You can then apply the enchantments from the custom scroll one by one to the same item or to different items. After the last enchantment is applied, the scroll is consumed.
